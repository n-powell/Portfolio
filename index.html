About Me

I come from a background of creating and experimenting. As a child I would take apart anything I could get my hands on. In the third grade we were tasked to build a valentines box to exchange cards with students. They suggested a shoe box, I made a box made from fence wood panels, nails, a door hindge, and finished it with a thick coat of white house paint. A shame the paint didnt dry by the next day for Valentines, but that was my first experience building something I was passionate about and realising that just when you think your'e finished, it may still not be ready.

My drive and desire to learn at EPICODUS comes from many years of tech hardware experience and pc building. Afer recieving an Assosiates of Science for Information Systems, I began experimenting with pc hardware and building gaming/entertainment computers for myself and friends.
